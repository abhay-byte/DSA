[HW] Aggressive Cows

Aggressive Cows
A Farmer has built a new long barn, with N (2 <= N <= 105) stalls. The stalls are located
along a straight line at positions x1…xN (0 <= xi <= 109). His C (2 <= C <= N) cows don't
like this barn layout and become aggressive towards each other once put into a stall.
To prevent the cows from hurting each other, the farmer wants to assign the cows to the
stalls, such that the minimum distance between any two of them is as large as possible.
What is the largest minimum distance?
Example
Input : C=3, N=5 [1, 2, 4, 8, 9]
Output : 3

[HW] Roti Prata

Roti Prata
IEEE is having its AGM next week and the president wants to serve cheese prata after
the meeting. The sub-committee members are asked to go to their food connections and
get P (P <= 1000) pratas packed for the function. The stall has L (L <= 50) cooks and
each cook has a rank R (1 <= R <= 8). A cook with a rank R can cook one prata in the
first R minutes, one more prata in the next 2R minutes, one more prata in the next 3R
minutes and so on (he can only cook a complete prata) - for instance, if the rank of a
cook is 2 then he will cook one prata in first 2 minutes, one more prata in the next 4 mins
and one more in the next 6 minutes hence in total 12 minutes he cooks 3 pratas, in 13
minutes also he can cook only 3 pratas as he does not have enough time for the 4th
prata. Design an algorithm to compute the minimum time to get the order done.
Example
○ Input : P=10, L=4 [1, 2, 3, 4]
○ Output : 12
